Humans

  • 2 dominant ethnicities, plus some smaller ones.
  • Etelan Humans occupy the southern and western regions of Ata. They are slightly shorter and have darker skin.
    • Etelan language is based on Finnish
  • Sonnern Humans occupy the eastern region of Ata. They are of medium height and fairer skin.
    • Sonnern language is based on German
