Smallfolk

Smallfolk

Fearless

It is said that smallfolk don't know fear. This appears to be the case, but only for island smallfolk, and it manifests differently for each individual. Some are genuinely courageous, while others seem plain oblivious to danger. On the other hand, the smallfolk of the Atan mountains are quite timid.
