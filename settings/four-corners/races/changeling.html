Changeling

  • Found on all 3 continents, in most major communities
  • Can live for up to a century, and don't suffer effects of old age until the very end
  • Strong dislike of changelings in both Ata and Gabroria
    • In both continents, changelings are rumored to steal human (or orc) infants and replace them with changelings, like mockingbirds
      • This superstition is probably absent in Verdania due to the scarcity of Elf infants
      • Based on some truth: doppelgangers, not changelings, do do this
      • Changelings reproduce just like any other race, but age does not affect their ability to have children
  • History and homeland of the race is unknown, since changelings rarely pass knowledge across generations
  • Some changelings love adopting new personas and never settle down, while others find a niche and may even reveal themselves to their community. Disproportionately many of them wind up as criminals due to their ability to evade recognition and impersonate others.
