Backgrounds

Here is a list of all backgrounds.

Dwarven Combat Training

You were trained in a Dwarven style of combat, perhaps as a soldier in a Dwarven army. You can speak, read, and write Dwarvish. You are also proficient with the battleaxe, handaxe, greataxe, light hammer, warhammer, maul, war pick, medium and heavy armor, and shields. When you roll initiative, you gain the following benefits for 1 minute:

  • While you are wielding one of the above weapons, you have advantage on Intimidation checks
  • While you are wearing such armor, you have advantage on Wisdom saving throws against being frightened

Dwarven Trade Training

You are experienced in a Dwarven trade. You can speak, read, and write Dwarvish, and pick one of the following benefits:

  1. You are proficient with brewer's supplies, and have expertise in History for the purpose of Intelligence checks relating to the identification of ale.
  2. You are proficient with jeweler's tools, and have expertise in History for the purpose of Intelligence checks relating to the origin of cut and uncut gems.
  3. You are proficient with mason's tools, and have expertise in History for the purpose of Intelligence checks relating to the origin of stonework.
  4. You are proficient with smith's tools, and have expertise in History for the purpose of Intelligence checks relating to the origin of forged arms and armor.
