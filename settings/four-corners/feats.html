Feats

Below are new feats.

Shielding Wings

Prerequisite: Dragonborn

Cost: 8 feat points

Your bulk makes flying difficult, but your tough wings make excellent shields. You gain the following benefits:

  • Your wings count as a shield, which you are proficient in.
  • Your base AC can be calculated as 10 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier, regardless of your armor.
  • If you also have the Shield Master feat, you can use the bonus action to shove each creature in a 15-foot cube instead of just one creature if you do so with your wings.

Soaring Wings

Prerequisite: Dragonborn

Cost: 8 feat points

Your wings are strong enough for flight. You gain a flying speed of 40 feet. If you also have the Shielding Wings feat or are wearing heavy armor, this flying speed is halved.

Recovered Soul

Prerequisite: Fey Elf

Cost: 3 feat points

Somehow, you have severed your soul's connection to the Feywild. Before taking this feat, discuss the means by which you accomplish this with your DM, as it may be narratively significant. You gain the following benefits and drawback:

  • You lose your Fey Soul racial trait.
  • You can expel your soul from your body to cast arcane eye without any components once with this trait. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for it. When you cast the spell in this way, you create a minature version of yourself instead of an eye. You regain the ability to cast it in this way when you finish a long rest.
  • As a side effect to your soul being loosely bound, it takes 1 less failed death saving throw for you to die.

Beast Friends

Prerequisite: Forest Gnome

Cost: 6 feat points

You gain the ability to make a special bond between yourself and a beast. The beast must be willing and Small or smaller. You can create the bond over the course of an hour, which may be during a short or long rest. You must be within 5 feet of the beast throughout the process as you bond with it. Afterwards, the beast gains the following benefits:

  • Whenever the beast finishes a short or long rest, it gains a number of temporary hit points equal to your level.
  • The beast learns a random spell on the Beast Spell table. It uses your Intelligence modifier as its spellcasting ability, it uses your level for cantrips, and it can cast the spell at will and without material components. If the spell requires verbal or somatic components, the beast's squeaks, tail wagging, or other behaviors are sufficient.

You can have a number of bonded beasts equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum one). If a beast dies, its bond breaks, and you regain the ability to bond a new beast 1 week after its death.

Divine Harbinger

Prerequisite: Aasimar, 3rd level

Cost: 8 feat points

Your Theurgic or Malefic Presence grants the following additional effects:

  • Spectral wings grow from your back, granting you a flying speed of 40 feet. These wings are made of brilliant light (theurgic) or murky darkness (malefic), but you choose their shape each time you manifest them.
  • Once on each of your turns, you can deal extra damage to one target when you deal damage to it with an attack or a spell. The extra damage equals your level, and is radiant (theurgic) or necrotic (malefic).

Air Genasi Magic

Prerequisite: Air Genasi

Cost: 2 feat points

Your ability to magically control air grows. You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you do, your Soul of Air trait grants one additional spell of your choice that you qualify for on the Air Genasi Spells table below.

To qualify for a spell, your level must be at least as high as the level shown in the table, and you must already know at least one spell from each lower level.

Mental Block

Prerequisite: Not immune to fear

Cost: 2 feat points

Your distrust of magic allows you to partially shield yourself from it. You gain the following benefit(s) and drawback:

  • You have advantage on Intelligence saving throws.
  • If you are a goliath, your Stone's Endurance trait can reduce psychic damage.
  • When you attempt to cast a spell, you must first succeed on a Constitution check. The DC is equal to 5 + the level of the spell. If you fail, it is as if you never attempted to cast the spell, but you cannot attempt to cast a spell again on this turn.

Many goliaths have this feat due to their stories of the fearful spirit Inoa'ole, who can possess anyone who opens their mind to magic.

Blessing of Amnat

Prerequisite: Dark Goblin or Servant of Amnat background

Cost: 8 feat points

Amnat has blessed you with special powers of shadows and of revenge. You gain the following benefits:

  • When you make a melee weapon attack while in darkness, you can magically target a creature that you can see even if it is outside your reach. The creature must also be in darkness, and must be within 30 feet of you. The creature cannot benefit from cover against this attack.
  • When you reduce an enemy to 0 hit points while you are in darkness, you magically gain temporary hit points equal to your level. These temporary hit points vanish early if you are exposed to bright light.
