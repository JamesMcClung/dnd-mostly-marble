Feats

Below are new feats.

Shielding Wings

Prerequisite: Dragonborn

Cost: 8 feat points

Your bulk makes flying difficult, but your tough wings make excellent shields. You gain the following benefits:

  • Your wings count as a shield, which you are proficient in.
  • Your base AC can be calculated as 10 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier, regardless of your armor.
  • If you also have the Shield Master feat, you can use the bonus action to shove each creature in a 15-foot cube instead of just one creature if you do so with your wings.

Soaring Wings

Prerequisite: Dragonborn

Cost: 8 feat points

Your wings are strong enough for flight. You gain a flying speed of 40 feet. If you also have the Shielding Wings feat or are wearing heavy armor, this flying speed is halved.

Recovered Soul

Prerequisite: Fey Elf

Cost: 3 feat points

Somehow, you have severed your soul's connection to the Feywild. Before taking this feat, discuss the means by which you accomplish this with your DM, as it may be narratively significant. You gain the following benefits and drawback:

  • You lose your Fey Soul racial trait.
  • You can expel your soul from your body to cast arcane eye without any components once with this trait. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for it. When you cast the spell in this way, you create a minature version of yourself instead of an eye. You regain the ability to cast it in this way when you finish a long rest.
  • As a side effect to your soul being loosely bound, it takes 1 less failed death saving throw for you to die.

Beast Friends

Prerequisite: Forest Gnome

Cost: 6 feat points

You gain the ability to make a special bond between yourself and a beast. The beast must be willing and Small or smaller. You can create the bond over the course of an hour, which may be during a short or long rest. You must be within 5 feet of the beast throughout the process as you bond with it. Afterwards, the beast gains the following benefits:

  • Whenever the beast finishes a short or long rest, it gains a number of temporary hit points equal to your level.
  • The beast learns a random spell on the Beast Spell table. It uses your Intelligence modifier as its spellcasting ability and your level for the purpose of cantrips. The beast can cast the spell at will unless otherwise specified, and it requires no material components. If the spell requires verbal or somatic components, the beast's squeaks, tail wagging, or other behaviors are sufficient.

You can have a number of bonded beasts equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum one). If a beast dies, its bond breaks, and you regain the ability to bond a new beast 1 week after its death.

Searing Presence

Prerequisite: Aasimar, 3rd level

Cost: 8 feat points

When you activate your Theurgic or Malefic Presence, you can choose to augment it with the following additional effects:

  • Spectral wings grow from your back, granting you a flying speed of 40 feet. These wings are made of brilliant light (theurgic) or murky darkness (malefic). You choose their shape when you select this feat.
  • Once on each of your turns, you can deal extra damage to one target when you deal damage to it with an attack or a spell. The extra damage equals your proficiency bonus, and is radiant (theurgic) or necrotic (malefic).
  • You take damage at the start of each of your turns. The damage equals 2 times the number of times you have already used this feature since you completed a long rest, and its type is radiant (theurgic) or necrotic (malefic).

Air Genasi Magic

Prerequisite: Air Genasi

Cost: 2 feat points

Your ability to magically control air grows. You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you do, your Soul of Air trait grants one additional spell of your choice that you qualify for on the Air Genasi Spells table below.

To qualify for a spell, your level must be at least as high as the level shown in the table, and you must already know at least one spell from each lower level.

Mental Block

Prerequisite: Not immune to fear

Cost: 2 feat points

Your distrust of magic allows you to partially shield yourself from it. You gain the following benefit(s) and drawback:

  • You have advantage on Intelligence saving throws.
  • If you are a goliath, your Stone's Endurance trait can reduce psychic damage.
  • When you attempt to cast a spell, you must first succeed on a Constitution check. The DC is equal to 5 + the level of the spell. If you fail, it is as if you never attempted to cast the spell, but you cannot attempt to cast a spell again on this turn

Many goliaths have this feat due to their stories of the fearful spirit Inoa'ole, who can possess anyone who opens their mind to magic.

Blessing of Amnat

Prerequisite: Dark Goblin or Servant of Amnat background

Cost: 8 feat points

Amnat has blessed you with special powers of shadows and of revenge. You gain the following benefits:

  • When you make a melee weapon attack while in darkness, you can magically target a creature that you can see even if it is outside your reach. The creature must also be in darkness, and must be within 30 feet of you. The creature cannot benefit from cover against this attack.
  • When you reduce an enemy to 0 hit points while you are in darkness, you magically gain temporary hit points equal to your level. These temporary hit points vanish early if you are exposed to bright light.

Amalgamated Chaos

Prerequisite: 3rd level, Atrophic Tiefling and chaotic alignment

Cost: 10 feat points

You have accepted—or succombed to—the urges in your head. You gain the following benefits:

  • You lose your Tiefling's Curse trait, but you retain your Atrophic Spells trait.
  • For you, thaumaturgy has a range of 150 feet, a duration of 10 minutes, and can be cast as a bonus action.
  • You can use Atrophic Spells to cast inflict wounds a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, instead of once, per long rest.
  • When you cast inflict wounds with your Atrophic Spells trait and hit, you can cause the target to wither as part of the spell. At the end of each of the target's turns, it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d10 necrotic damage. The withering ends on a successful save or after 1 minute.
  • When you cast crown of madness with your Atrophic Spells trait, you can target up to a number of creatures equal to your proficiency bonus, and you can use a single action or a bonus action to maintain control over all of them.

Amalgamated Order

Prerequisite: 3rd level, Leguleian Tiefling and lawful alignment

Cost: 10 feat points

You have accepted—or succombed to—the urges in your head. You gain the following benefits:

  • You lose your Tiefling's Curse trait, but you retain your Leguleian Spells trait.
  • For you, thaumaturgy has a range of 150 feet, a duration of 10 minutes, and can be cast as a bonus action.
  • You can use Leguleian Spells to cast hellish rebuke a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, instead of once, per long rest.
  • When you cast hellish rebuke with your Atrophic Spells trait and the target fails its saving throw, you can place an interdiction on the target as part of the spell. While under the interdiction, the target cannot attack you or target you with harmful abilities or magical effects. The target may make a Charisma saving throw at the end of each of its turns. The interdiction ends on a successful save or after 1 minute.
  • When you cast zone of truth with your Leguleian Spells trait, you can also use Leguleian Spells to cast command at will targeting any number of creatures within the zone.

Peak of Corruption

Prerequisite: Tiefling, Tiefling's Curse trait

Cost: 8 feat points

Your Curse warps your body and mind beyond what is normally survivable. Your Tiefling's Curse manifests in an additional way: you have the indefinite madness, "sometimes, the voice in my head takes control."

When remove curse is cast on you, you must make a Constitution saving throw against the spell save DC. On a success, your Tiefling's Curse is not lifted.

Finally, choose one of the following benefits:

  1. You grow bat-like wings, granting you a flying speed of 30 feet. This flying speed is halved while you wear heavy armor.
  2. Your skin hardens to chitinous or osseous plates. Your base AC can be calculated as 10 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier, regardless of your armor.
  3. Your tail, horns, and/or claws are empowered by your Curse and you can use them to make unarmed strikes. They count as magical natural weapons with the finesse and light properties, have a damage die of 1d8, and deal bludgeoning damage (tail), piercing damage (horns), or slashing damage (claws). Once on each of your turns, you can cause a creature hit by one of your unarmed strikes to take an extra 1d8 fire damage (Leguleian tiefling) or necrotic damage (Atrophic tiefling).

You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you do, choose a different benefit.
