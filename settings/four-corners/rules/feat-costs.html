Feat Points

This is a WIP system to replace the usual method of acquiring feats.

Players gain 2 feat points with each level, and may spend feat points at the end of a long rest or upon leveling up. A feat of median strength should have a cost of 8.

Half-feats no longer grant ASIs. These feats are marked by a * below.

Costs take training into consideration; feats that solely grant proficiency are less expensive.
