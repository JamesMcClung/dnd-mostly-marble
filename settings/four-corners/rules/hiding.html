Hiding

A creature automatically knows your location at all times unless you are either Hidden from or Unknown to that creature.

Unknown

You are Unknown to a creature unless it notices or otherwise suspects your presence. This requires a (minimal) conscious effort on the part of the creature.

Examples of Being Unknown

  • You are not Unknown to a dog tracking your scent, a diligent guard on duty, a dragon who is woken by your loud footsteps, or someone who recognizes you in a crowd
  • You may be Unknown to a drunken guard, a sleeping dragon, or a stranger in a busy street

Hidden

You are Hidden from a creature if that creature is unable to keep track of where you are. You cease to be Hidden from a creature that sees or hears you. You automatically reveal your location if you attack or cast a spell, unless the spell has neither verbal components nor noticeable effects emanating from you.

Examples of Being Hidden

  • You are not Hidden from a guard that hears your armor clanking, or an earth elemental who senses your vibrations
  • You may be Hidden from a guard whose back is turned, a sleeping dog, a passing caravan, or a bandit whose captain just shouted your location
Hide DC. Your Hide DC determines how difficult you are to detect while Hidden. The DC is equal to your passive Stealth by default. A creature whose passive Perception exceeds your Hide DC automatically detects you. If you want to remain Hidden, you must make a Stealth check the first time on a turn you move without sneaking or crawling; the result of the check becomes your Hide DC for up to 1 minute. Sneaking and crawling both take an extra foot of movement for each foot traveled.

Hide

The Hide action allows you to make a Dexterity (Stealth) check and attempt to become Hidden from creatures that can't see you. This check becomes your Hide DC for up to 1 hour. Note that this is typically the only way to regain the Hidden status during combat.

Search

The Search action allows you to make a Wisdom (Perception) check to identify the location of Hidden creatures. The DC to detect a Hidden creature is its Hide DC. If the manner of a creature's hiding would cause you to have disadvantage on this check and you don't already have it, its Hide DC is increased by 5 instead for the purpose of this check.
