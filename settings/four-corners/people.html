People

A list of well-known people, organized by category.

Scholars

Juvela Cherny

A female human historian. Sparked minor field of soulhistory by doing case studies on people who claim to have visions of past lives and comparing those visions to real history.

Andre "the Bold" Hurst

A male human explorer and anthropologist. Disappeared on his 183 SK expedition in the central Gabrorian mountains seeking to interrogate stone giants regarding their relation with goliaths.
