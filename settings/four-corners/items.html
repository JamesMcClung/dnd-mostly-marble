Items

Below is a list of all new items.

Tower Shield

Mundane armor (shield)
Cost: 40 gp
An extremely tall, heavy shield. You are only proficient with this shield if you are proficient with both shields and heavy armor, unless you specifically gain proficiency with tower shields through some other means.
While wielding this shield, you have half cover against ranged attacks and for the purposes of Dexterity saving throws. You also have disadvantage on Stealth checks, cannot ride a mount, and unless you have a Strength score of 17 or higher, your speed is reduced by 5 feet.

Greatbow

Mundane martial weapon
Cost: 75 gp
A massive bow that takes a great deal of strength to wield. It is 6 feet tall, weighs 4 pounds and deals 1d12 piercing damage on a hit. It has the ammunition (range 150/600), heavy, and two-handed properties. Attack and damage rolls made with this bow use Strength instead of Dexterity.
