The World

There are three continents, one pseudocontinent, and countless islands of varying sizes. The continents loosely split the world ocean into three sections. The following table gives an overview of the world, but see below for more detailed descriptions.

Continents

Ata

Main page: Ata
Ata is the second-largest continent, roughly shaped like a 'σ'. A horizontal mountain range divides most of the continent into northern and southern halves. North of the mountains are taiga and then tundra. The bulk of the continent below the mountains is grassland. Deciduous tree cover increases closer to the coast. The east coast is exceptionally fertile, and Humans and Orcs have fought many wars over it.
Humans currently occupy most of the south and parts of the northern coast, leaving the slopes of the mountains to the Orcs.

Verdania

Main page: Verdania
Verdania, the smallest continent, is dominated by colossal shield volcano called Verdoa. The sea enters the volcano's caldera in the north, filling it and giving Verdania its lunate shape.

Islands, Isles, & Archipelagos

Dragon Island

Home to the Dragonborn and Kobolds, and often visited by dragons. A lush jungle covers the island except for the rims of three large calderas. The volcanos on the island itself are believed to be dormant, but earthquakes are still common and new isles are always on the rise to the south.
The isles and rocky outcroppings in the surrounding waters make landing a large ship here impossible. The steep cliffs on the shore provide a second natural barrier to invasion. The volcanic rock is sharp and crumbly, so the only way up is to fly or navigate the many lava tubes. The latter option could result in the discovery of a Kobold den before promptly ending.
