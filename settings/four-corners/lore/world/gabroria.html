Gabroria

Gabroria is the largest continent. A y-shaped mountain range spans most of the continent along the north/south axis.

Civilizations and Settlements

Norrebe

  • Rock gnomes
  • Built in a canyon network over a major river
  • Trades with dwarves
  • Main producer of high-tech stuff

Tiris Vale

  • Rot gnomes
  • Built in a valley with access to cave system below
    • Cave system is believed to be isolated from other caves, but has horrors inside
    • Rare mineral grows in this cave like stalagmites
  • Everyone is extremely diseased/half-undead
    • Backfired attempt at clearing horrors from cave
  • Necromancy capital of the world
  • Literal undead are used as servants and warriors against cave horrors

Sand Gnome City

  • Hidden in desert, not open to outsiders
  • Rarely trade with outsiders, and when they do, it's almost exclusively with rock gnomes
  • <redacted>
  • Only source of mindmetal, which is rarely exported
  • Traverse desert via sand sailboats

Granite Dwarf Capital

  • Amazing architecture
