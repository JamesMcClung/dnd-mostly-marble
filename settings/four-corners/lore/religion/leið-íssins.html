Leið Íssins

  • Very old cultish religion with a small following of silver dwarves
  • Believe that the ice once covered the whole earth, and that it will again soon
  • Only two "divine" figures: Ísvængur the Prophet and Leviathan the Destroyer
    • Prophet's tomb is a sacred place, but is in the underwater ruins of Vistad
    • Leviathan believed to be harbinger of next ice age
  • Many practitioners try to reach the Prophet's tomb
    • High demand for magic items that facilitate underwater breathing; some merchants sell fakes
    • Not uncommon for dwarves to die from drowning or the cold, sometimes due to faulty magic items
  • Meditate every morning for 1 minute underwater and keep track of its temperature
  • Try to get an underwater tomb yourself, as that is the only place safe from the coming ice age

History

  • Initiated over 3,600 years ago by Ásmundur "ÍsvængurIcewing" Ásgeir, its prophet
    • Ísvængur literally had wings of ice
    • Vistad was built at the place of his death, on an island in the Silver Sound
  • Vistad spread the religion and formed the First Silver Empire, led by priests
    • Location of Vistad was amenable to controlling trade in the sound
  • After ~500 years, Leviathan sank Vistad into the sound with unprecedented flooding
    • The Empire fell, but the religion survived
