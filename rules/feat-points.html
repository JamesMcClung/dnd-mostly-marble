Feat Points

This system replaces the usual method of acquiring feats.
Players gain 2 feat points each level, and may spend feat points at the end of a long rest or upon leveling up.
Half-feats no longer grant ability score increases.
A typical feat should cost 8 points, or 4 points for a half-feat.
