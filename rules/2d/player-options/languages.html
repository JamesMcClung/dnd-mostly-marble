Languages

Languages

Languages are used to communicate. Most sentient creatures are proficient in at least one language.

List of Languages

Language Proficiency

Being proficient in a language means you can communicate fluently using that language in any of its forms. If the language is spoken, you can speak it; if the language has a writing system, you can read and write it.

Telepathy

Telepathy is the ability to communicate mind-to-mind to another creature. Features that grant telepathy specify how it can be used. There are many variants:
  • Some forms of telepathy require sight.
  • Some forms of telepathy are one-way, while others allow a creature to respond in kind.
  • Some forms of telepathy involve the transmission of images or ideas, while others use words (and thus require the use of a mutually understood language to be useful).
