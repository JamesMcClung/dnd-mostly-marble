Skills

Skills

When you make an ability check using a skill, add your proficiency in that skill to the check. Your proficiency in a skill equals your proficiency bonus if you have proficiency with that skill, or twice your proficiency bonus if you have expertise with that skill.

List of Skills

Acrobatics

Ability: Dexterity
Acrobatics is for balancing and flexibility of the body. It is explicitly used in jumping.

Arcana

Ability: Intelligence
Arcana is for knowledge of, and reasoning about, magic. It is explicitly used in the Recognize Spell reaction and the Study action.

Animal Handling

Ability: Wisdom
Animal handling is for interacting with animals, especially mounts.

Athletics

Ability: Strength
Athletics is for feats of sport and strength. It is explicitly used in climbing, jumping, grappling, and several unarmed attacks.

Deception

Ability: Charisma
Deception is for keeping a straight face and convincing people of facts you know to be false. It is explicitly used in the Taunt action.

History

Ability: Intelligence
History is for knowledge of past people, places, and events. It is explicitly used in the Study action.

Insight

Ability: Wisdom
Insight is for judging people's nature and intent. It is explicitly used in the Search action.

Intimidation

Ability: Charisma
Intimidation is for scaring others and making yourself appear more threatening. It is explicitly used in the Frighten action.

Investigation

Ability: Intelligence
Investigation is for finding information based on knowledge you already have and readily available resources. It is explicitly used in the Search action.

Medicine

Ability: Intelligence
Medicine is for diagnosing and treating health problems. It is explicitly used in the Heal action.

Nature

Ability: Intelligence
Nature is for knowledge of plants, beasts, and the environment. It is explicitly used in the Study action.

Perception

Ability: Wisdom
Perception is for noticing things using your physical senses. It is explicitly used in the Search action and hidden condition.

Performance

Ability: Charisma
Performance is for acting a part or playing a musical instrument. It is explicitly used in the Taunt action.

Persuasion

Ability: Charisma
Persuasion is for inspiring others and converting them to your point of view. It is explicitly used in the Taunt action.

Religion

Ability: Intelligence
Religion is for knowledge of religious rites, tenets, and scripture. It is explicitly used in the Study action.

Stealth

Ability: Dexterity
Stealth is for sneaking around. It is explicitly used in the Hide action.

Survival

Ability: Wisdom
Survival is for following tracks, navigating on foot, and obtaining resources in the wilderness. It is explicitly used in the Search action.

Sleight of Hand

Ability: Dexterity
Sleight of hand is for subtle hand tricks and other acts requiring fine motor control.
